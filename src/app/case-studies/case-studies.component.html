Turning Luggage into Stories -

RIMOWA Campaign

Client Name

RIMOWA

Role

Microsite Design, Content Writing, Campaign Strategy

The Brief

RIMOWA wanted to create a campaign that connected emotionally with their audience — showing that every RIMOWA luggage isn’t just a travel bag, it’s part of a personal journey.

Needed a temporary campaign site to run alongside their global marketing.

Wanted the site to tell stories, not just show products.

Needed a focus on brand storytelling without distracting from RIMOWA’s main website.

Strategic Insights

We built a microsite dedicated to the campaign “My Rimowa Story.” Microsites are perfect for short-term campaigns because they keep the message laser-focused, without cluttering the main brand

Dedicated Team

Focused designers + writers worked closely with RIMOWA’s marketing team.

Content Strategy

Every line of text was tied back to the brand’s heritage and premium identity.

End-to-End Delivery

From concept, sitemap, content, to microsite launch.

The Execution Plan

  • Content Writing

    Crafted narrative-driven copy that connects customer journeys with RIMOWA products.

  • Creative Direction

    Designed visuals aligned with RIMOWA’s premium identity and global aesthetic.

  • Storytelling First

    Focused on experiences and emotions rather than direct selling, making each page feel human.

  • Microsite Structure

    Developed a 4-page sitemap (Home / My Rimowa Story / World of Rimowa / Stores) to keep the campaign focused and easy to navigate.
